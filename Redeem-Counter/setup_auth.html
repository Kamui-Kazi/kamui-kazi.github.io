Redeem Counter Bot - Setup Guide - Authenticate Bots

The next step is to autherize your bots, this is so that your bots can connect to twitch.

There are 9 steps to authenticating your bots, don't worry it's all done through a menu:

  1. run the file "run.py"
  2. on the menu screen, select option 2
  3. wait for the console to stop printing text
    4. In the text there should be a link, either use it from there or use this link.
    http://localhost:4343/oauth?scopes=user:read:chat%20user:write:chat%20user:bot%20channel:bot%20channel:read:redemptions
  4. visit the link in your browser while logged into twitch as your stream account
  5. visit the link in your browser while logged into twitch as your bot account
    6. To make this easier you can login to your bot account in a private/incognito window, or in an unused browser like edge.
    Doing so will let you stay logged in as your stream account on your main browser window.
  6. Both times you visit the links you should be prompted to allow a list of permissions, click allow.
  7. You should see text in the console that indicates tokens were added to the database for the user IDs of both accounts
  8. press ctrl+c to bring you back to the menu, or kill the bots and relaunch
  9. when you are back to the menu screen you should be ready for the next step
