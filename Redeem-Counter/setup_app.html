Redeem Counter Bot - Setup Guide - Twitch App

The first thing you need to do is create a twitch app.

There are 13 steps to creating a twitch app:

  1. As the streamer's account visit "https://dev.twitch.tv/console"
  2. On the left panel select Applications
  3. On the right side of the Apps page click "+ Register Your Application"
  4. Enter a name for your application, doesn't matter what it is.
  5. In the "OAuth Redirect URLs" add the url "http://localhost:4343/oauth/callback" don't press the "Add" button
  6. For "Category" select "Chat Bot" this doesn't technicly matter but it's best to stay orginized.
  7. For "Client Type" leave it as "Confidential"
  8. Complete the Captcha
  9. Hit the "Create" button
  10. At the bottom of the page, save your Client ID, we will need this
  11. click the "New Secret" button
  12. click "ok" to say that yes you are sure you want to make a new secret
  13. Save the new "Client Secret" we need this

The next step is to set up the enviroment variables next
