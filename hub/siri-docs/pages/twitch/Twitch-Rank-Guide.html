Sharing Trauma

If you see someone Trauma Dumping please remove the message ASAP. It causes the stream to feel very awkward for a bit and discourages chatters to chat.

Simple Copy-Paste: Send as a Warning
(@User) The Siri staff team is very sorry to hear about the tough time that you're going through currently. But, the chat is not a great place to be discussing this topic. Our reasoning for this is other chatters may possibly having a trigger for these topics, and we want to keep this chat safe for everyone. If you need help please reach out to your local hotlines. Thank You.

NOTE: Most users have whispers disabled, so they can only receive whispers directly from people they follow. This can be sent in a Warning instead.

Boundaries

If you can tell a chatter in the chat is making me uncomfortable you can send me a message on discord or ping me in the staff channel as deal with them as you see fit.

Bullying

Any rude/hateful comments are to be removed immediately and user timed out/banned depending on what was said.

Racial/Homophobic Slurs

First Offense: Ban and Report to Twitch

Soliciting Subs/Gifts/Skins

First Offense: Delete Message & Warn
Second Offense: Timeout 10 minutes
Third Offense: Ban

Spamming

Spam is typically considered 3+ messages that are the same or similar within 2-3 minutes.
First Offense: Warning
Second Offense: Timeout 3-5 minutes
Third Offense: Timeout 10 minutes
Forth Offense: Ban

Underage

Ban them, then report them!

Not Otherwise Classified

Use your best judgement and be fair.
Typical escalation is Delete & Warn > Timeout > Timeout Longer > Ban
First Offense/Minor: Delete Message and issue warning if needed.
Second Offense/Moderate: 60-300 seconds (1-5 minutes) Timeout
Third Offense/Major: 600-1,800 seconds (10-30 minutes) Timeout
Fourth Offense/Severe: Ban

REMEMBER TO LOG YOUR REASON IN MOD COMMENTS FOR ANYTHING MORE THAN DELETING MESSAGES, INCLUDING WARNINGS
