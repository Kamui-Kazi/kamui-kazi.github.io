English only

This is an English only chat. This makes everything easier on our moderators.

Sometimes rules are not completely read before entering chat. Here is the breakdown and proper response:

  • 1st Offense: Timeout for 60-100s
  • 2nd Offense: Timeout for 300s (5m) - @ the user and give a warning
  • 3rd Offense: Ban

No hate speech/bullying and politics

This rule is a bit difficult because of the culture of the community. Insults thrown toward the broadcaster are fine, unless they are targeting a specific type of person and not just the broadcaster.

Hate speech

Hate speech refers to communication that belittles, threatens, or discriminates against individuals or groups based on attributes such as race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality. It is intended to incite hostility, violence, or prejudice.

Here is the breakdown and proper response:

  • Jokes made that are in bad taste, typically dark humor, will receive a timeout for 300s.
  • Racial slurs will receive an immediate ban.
  • Gender is a middle ground, considering the broadcaster is very close to gender-neutral. Light-hearted jokes are fine and can be ignored. The territory that steps into not fine would be directly insulting different genders/sexualities.

    “Ha ur gay” ← This is fine

    “Youre fucking gay dude” ← Not fine

    This will often be left up to moderator discretion and will receive the following punishments:
    • 1st Offense: 120s (5m) timeout + warning (if it seemed as if it were in a joking manner)
    • 2nd Offense (Or after intense insult): Ban

Politics

As for politics. Politics can be spoken of in small doses. If the broadcaster brings up something political, the chat practically has a pass to continue the conversation.
However, promoting of a political addenda is looked down upon and will receive a timeout depending on the aggressiveness of the promotion.
Here is the breakdown and proper response:

  • Offenses will be handled with a 120s (1m) - 300s (5m) timeout. And an general announcement will be made to chat.
  • Bans can be dealt if the aggression is too high (up to mod interpretation) or if the chatter can restated the same thing many times over.

Try Not To Spam

This is another difficult topic. There is a difference between welcome and unwelcome spam.
Some broadcasters will ask chat to spam something. This is perfectly okay, especially in my spammy ass little chat of mine.
An example of bad spam would be the repasting of the same exact question/sentence over and over again. I consider it spam after the 4th time of seeing the message.
This can be met with a timeout of moderator choosing.
If the moderator watching sees them come back with the same question after timeout or sees that the question is controversial or rude after timeout, the chatter can be banned.

No Self Advertisement

You will be hunted down by Narcos

(he has rabies)

Self advertisement is seen as “you should come watch my stream”
Mentioning that someone also streams is not a bad thing. We as content creators all support each other, however only chatting to bring attention to yourself is a bad practice and disrespectful to everyone there.

  • 1st Offense: 60s (1m) timeout.
  • 2nd Offense: 300s (5) timeout + warning
  • 3rd Offense: Ban
